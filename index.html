About Us

derdawo.github.io/Nonogram is a static Webpage which features a classical japanese game: nonogram

"Nonograms, also known as Paint by Numbers, Picross, Griddlers, Pic-a-Pix, and various other names, are picture logic puzzles in which cells in a grid must be colored or left blank according to numbers at the side of the grid to reveal a hidden picture. Nonograms were named after Non Ishida, one of the two inventors of the puzzle." Wikipedia

This Page uses Cookies to track your Progress in the game. This feature is completely optional and not necessary to acces the page.
Without the usage of cookies, everything will be unlocked immediatly. With the usage of cookies, you will have to start at Level 1 in each category and work your way through the levels.
